# Tamil Nadu Assembly Election 2026 Analysis

## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of the 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Election using SQL and Power BI. The objective was to uncover meaningful electoral patterns, voter participation trends, and constituency-level representation changes through data-driven storytelling.

The project was developed as part of the **Codebasics Resume Project Challenge 2026**, focusing on transforming raw election data into actionable insights through analytics and visualization.

---

## Problem Statement

Most election discussions focus on political debates and opinions. This project takes a different approach by analyzing publicly available Election Commission of India (ECI) data to identify key electoral trends and present them through a clear, fact-based narrative.

The analysis focuses on three major themes:

* Party Performance Analysis
* Voter Participation & Turnout
* Constituency-Level Representation

## Research Questions Addressed

### 1. Vote Share Story

How was voter support distributed among political parties in the 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Election?

### 2. Turnout Story

How did voter participation vary across Tamil Nadu regions?

### 3. Constituency Representation Story

How did constituency-level outcomes shape Assembly representation in 2026?

---

## Key Findings

### Party Performance

* TVK recorded the highest vote count in the election.
* TVK emerged as the largest party in the Assembly.
* Vote share and seat share were not always proportional across parties.

### Voter Participation

* Tamil Nadu recorded an average voter turnout of 73.04%.
* Central Region reported the highest voter turnout.
* Most regions recorded turnout above 70%.

### Constituency Representation

* TVK secured 107 constituencies.
* Multiple parties retained Assembly representation.
* Constituency-level outcomes resulted in significant shifts in political representation.
